The idea behind#

The idea that software should be shared and freely accessible for everyone lies at the core of programming. Sharing software allows individuals to learn from the experience of others, to avoid common mistakes and consequently - to write software faster and better, advancing our understanding of the world. Therefore, I, allowing others to learn and learning by myself, want to share my Github with those interested in changing our lives!
